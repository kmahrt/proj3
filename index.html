Kathleen's Media Bookshelf

Hello! My name is Kathleen and this website documents all of my favorite books that I have ever read. I love to read thrillers, romances, fantasy, and nonfiction. This website is a way for me to organize my thoughts and to document books I have read. I will include all YouTube videos I have made on these subjects for easy access to my entire thoughts on each book I mention. I hope, in the future, to expand my horizons and read more of different genres. I also love to watch movies and shows, and would like to keep track of all that I liked/disliked. If you have the same taste as me, you may even find inspiration for your next piece of media!
